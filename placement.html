The Placement Cell of Government Engineering College Idukki (GECI) plays a vital role in guiding students towards successful careers.

It provides training in aptitude, technical skills, and soft skills to prepare students for recruitment drives.

The cell also invites reputed companies from various sectors, creating opportunities for internships and placements.

With consistent support and industry collaborations, the Placement Cell ensures that students are well-equipped to excel in the competitive job market.
